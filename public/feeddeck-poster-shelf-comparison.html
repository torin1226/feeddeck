What changes vs. before:
  • HeroSection (493 lines) deleted. No more 100vh fixed hero. The poster carousel is the homepage.
  • Cards expand on focus with spring easing. Neighbors dim to 0.78, far cards to 0.55. Mixed aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16) sit on the same track.
  • Glass info panel floats below the focused card. Title, meta, tags, and action buttons all live here. Replaces the hero's bottom-left content stack.
  • Theatre is now a route. Clicking the focused card calls navigate('/watch/:id'). Back button returns to the homepage with carousel state preserved. /watch can be deep-linked or shared.
  • BrowseSection kept below. Continue Watching, Top 10, additional category rows still discoverable on scroll. The full-viewport feel applies above the fold.
  • What's still there from current state: peek-row preview of next category, progress dots, autoplay video preview on focused card.
  • 5c.2b features NOT yet active here: only one category at a time, no infinite pool, no category dividers, peek-row click doesn't hydrate.

What Push B adds on top of Push A:
  • Flat pool. homeStore exposes a single ordered pool[]. Every item carries _cat/_catLabel. No more "category 1 ends, category 2 starts on a separate row."
  • Vertical divider pill between sections. Glass capsule with rotated label and an arrow that hints at the next category. Arrow keys / scroll skip past it automatically.
  • Header cross-fade. The shelf header label fades from "Live Music" → "My Subscriptions" (250ms) when the focused card crosses the divider. The mock shows the moment of transition — both labels visible at 50/50.
  • Windowed dots. Only ~11–15 dots render, centered on the active card. Far dots fade to ~30% and shrink. Solves the "200 dots in a row" problem when the pool is long.
  • Peek-row click hydrates. Tapping the peek-row triggers a fetch for that category, appends to the pool, and jumps the carousel to the first card of the new category.
  • Background refill. When the user is within 3 cards of the pool's end, an idle-callback hits /api/homepage for a fresh batch. Items append without disrupting scroll position.
  • What's still the same: /watch/:id route, info panel, BrowseSection below, focus expansion, glass tokens.
Before · what hurts
  • Hero eats 100vh for one item
  • Hero strip is a 6-thumb redundant carousel
  • Theatre mode swells in-place — no shareable URL
  • Two GalleryShelf rows compete with hero
  • Heavy HeroSection.jsx (493 lines, 6 stores)
Push A · scope
  • Remove HeroSection from HomePage
  • GalleryShelf becomes full-viewport (~85vh)
  • Migrate playback to /watch/:id (rebuild on top of VideoDetailPage skeleton — close 3 P1 gaps: HLS.js, queue autoadvance, server-fallback ID lookup)
  • Card click → navigate('/watch/:id')
  • Preserve carousel scroll/focus on back nav
  • Keep BrowseSection below
Push B · scope
  • Refactor homeStore to flat pool[] with _cat markers
  • Lazy-load: 2 categories on init, append on approach to end
  • Vertical divider pill component
  • Skip-divider logic for arrow keys + scroll
  • Header cross-fade on category boundary
  • Windowed dots (~15 centered)
  • Peek-row click hydrates target category
  • Background refill on near-exhaustion
